Where to Live? Compare Two Cities

Motivation:
The data comes from USNews & Wikipedia that includes weather, house, commute, crime, population, salary and sunshine duration information. I chose these datasets because I thought it originally thought it would be interesting to compare sunlight duration with wellbeing or happiness scores but couldn't find anything related -- so I ended up with this equally cool dataset and with this information, you can easily compare cities and highlight specific ones if you want to! One of my favorite findings is that Seattle only has 1.76 hours of sunlight in December, which is quite depressing :(. Also, Los Angeles has comparably small average salary than expected. Larger salaries also tend to entail a longer commute, upwards of 30 minutes. There's many more interesting things to explore for yourself! Be sure hover over the dots for extra info!
